About Me

I am a recording and mixing engineer, sound designer, and composer based in Manchester, U.K.
I have gained a diploma from SEM (formerly Manchester Midi School), where I could deepen my knowledge
in Electronic Music Composition, Advanced Music Production, and Audio Engineering. I have worked as a
professional singer and actor for over fifteen years and have been doing audio engineering and production
since 2016. It was at this time that I decided to invest my time and resources into making a career in the music
industry. In 2021, I graduated in Audio Engineering and Sound Production at Futureworks, University Education for
The Creative Industries, as a First-Class Student. Lately, I have been working as a Post-production freelance,
handling the sound design of several professional feature films, short films, and commercials.

Alongside my work in the audio industry, I am also passionate about technology and have recently started to explore
the world of software development. I am eager to broaden my skill set and am currently learning how to become a
Full Stack Engineer. My goal is to combine my expertise in audio engineering with full stack development, enabling
me to create innovative solutions that bridge the gap between sound design and web technologies. I am excited about
the opportunities that lie ahead as I continue to develop my skills in both fields.

Places I'd Like to Visit

Greek Islands

The Greek Islands are a dream destination with their crystal-clear waters, whitewashed villages, and rich history
offering a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. Each island has its own unique charm, from the lively
atmosphere of Mykonos to the serene landscapes of Santorini, making them ideal for both relaxation and exploration.

Sydney

Sydney is a must-visit destination for its iconic landmarks like the Sydney Opera House
and Harbour Bridge,offering breathtaking views and cultural experiences. The city's stunning beaches
vibrant nightlife, and rich culinary scene make it an ideal spot for both relaxation and adventure.

Hawaii

Hawaii is a paradise of lush landscapes, pristine beaches, and vibrant culture, offering unforgettable experiences
from surfing on world-famous waves to exploring volcanic wonders. The islands' unique blend of natural beauty,
rich traditions, and warm aloha spirit makes Hawaii a perfect destination for both adventure and relaxation.
